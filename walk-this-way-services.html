Services

We offer exercise and socialization for your
best friend. Our group hikes give your dog a
chance to explore the outdoors with a pack of
furry friends. Your dog will be picked up for
a fun, group hike in the hills of Marin and
returned to you after the walk. We understand
that each dog is differeent and are happy to meet
whatever needs you and your pet may have.

Our services include group hikes, individual walks
and pet sitting.

Group Walks

Group walks introduce your dog to a social
environment where he or she can interact and make
friends with other dogs. Being part of a pack
stimulates your dog mentally and teaches him or her
dog etiquette. Give your best friend a chance
to make new friends by sending him or her on daily
group walks! Group walks last one hour.

Individual Walks

Individual walks give your dog a chance for some
one-on-one time with our Walk This Way dog walkers.
Great for puppies, older dogs or dogs with special
needs, the Individual Walk is a great way to give
your dog lots of TLC. Individual walks last 25 mins.

Pet Sitting: Your Home

This allows your dog to stay in the comfort of his
or her own home and still receive love and care while
you're away. Pet Sitting includes morning and evening
walks, food, water, any medications necessary,
picking up mail, watering plants and, of course,
lots of TLC.

Pet Sitting: Our Home

If you would prefer, we can take care of your dog
overnight at our house. Your dog will be cared for
completely with lots of love, two walks a day, and we
care of any special needs.
